Distributed trust graphs let autonomous agents route decisions through verifiable attestations instead of opaque reputation scores. EigenTrust-style convergence on sparse graphs resists sybil clusters when pre-trusted seeds are curated.

Key insight: nanopayments make per-attestation verification economically viable at sub-cent granularity.
