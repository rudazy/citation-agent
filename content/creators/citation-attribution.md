Research agents should pay creators at citation time, not at subscription time. Each cited passage triggers a micro-royalty split: 70% to the creator wallet, 30% to the platform facilitator.

Exclusive attribution twist: responses must include paid citation provenance so downstream systems can verify which sources were actually purchased, not hallucinated.
