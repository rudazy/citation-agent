Berachain liquidity is increasingly sticky: 62% of TVL in the top dozen pools has remained deployed for more than 60 days, up from 41% at launch. BGT emissions still dominate yield narratives, but routing analysis shows a growing share of rewards recycled into bribe markets rather than sold at emission.

Pool-level depth matters for agents routing size. Stable-stable pairs carry the tightest spreads but thin quickly above $250k notional. HONEY-weighted volatile pairs absorb flow during NFT and gaming token launches, then mean-revert within 72 hours as emissions decay.

This analysis covers LP wallet cohorts, bribe efficiency ratios, and cross-pool migration when new gauge weights activate. Includes a ranked table of pools by 7-day net inflow and implied slippage at $100k, $500k, and $1M trade sizes.
